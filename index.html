About Track Dog Racing

Two decades of Miata expertise, one focused mission — make your car faster.

Track Dog Racing started in a garage in 2003 with a simple premise: Miata owners deserve a dedicated, expert source for performance parts. Not a generic catalog — a focused shop that actually knows the car.

The Track Dog Difference

Every brand we carry has been evaluated by our team — and in most cases, driven at the track. We don't list parts we haven't verified fit. Our catalog is smaller than the big box stores by design, because quality always beats quantity.

Generations We Support

We cover every generation of the Miata / MX-5: the original NA (1990–1997), the NB (1999–2005), the NC (2006–2015), and the current ND (2016+). Whether you drive a bone-stock autocross car or a full-build track machine, we have what you need.
